Bottling and flavoring of brew no. 2.

##### Flavoring

Strawberry and mint flavor:
1. 450 g of strawberries
2. 1 tbsp of sugar
3. 150 ml of water
4. pack of mint leaves (6 per bottle)

Yields 6x 0,5 L bottles of kombucha.

##### Notes

Since brew no. 2 contained a very promising amount of carbonation,
the bottles are currently stored in the sink covered by a tea towel for
safety reasons.
